Community Guidelines

Keeping discovery a safe and professional space for everyone

Professional Conduct

Always maintain a respectful and professional tone when interacting with other users. Harassment, discrimination, and hate speech are strictly prohibited.

Authentic Interactions

Members are expected to portray their professional backgrounds authentically. Connections should be established to foster meaningful discourse and mutual growth.

Platform Integrity

Do not spam users, scrape data aggressively, distribute malicious links, or create multiple fake accounts to manipulate search discoverability.
